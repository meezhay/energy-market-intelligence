# Energy Investment Intelligence for Sub-Saharan Africa

## Overview

This project analyzes electricity access, renewable energy adoption, economic indicators, and investment-related metrics across Sub-Saharan Africa to identify countries with strong potential for energy sector investment.

The project demonstrates an end-to-end analytics workflow using SQL, Python, and Power BI.

---

## Business Problem

An international energy company wants to identify attractive African markets for renewable energy expansion.

Which countries should be prioritized?

---

## Objectives

- Analyze electricity access trends
- Compare renewable energy indicators
- Evaluate market attractiveness
- Visualize country performance
- Generate business recommendations

---
